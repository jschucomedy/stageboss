Who We Are

A Touring
Entertainment
Powerhouse

Main Event Comedy Entertainment is a national comedy production company built for the road. We produce and promote stand-up comedy shows across the United States — from intimate comedy club runs to casino headline dates and major theater productions.

Founded by comedians and producers Jason Schuster and Phil Medina, we understand what it takes to put on a great show because we've been doing it from the stage up. Every production is handled with the same standard: professional, polished, and packed.

Based in Baltimore, Maryland and Los Angeles, California — we operate coast to coast with a network of 333+ venues and counting.

What We Offer

Show Packages

From intimate club nights to full theater productions — Main Event Comedy Entertainment handles everything. Tell us what you need and we'll build the right show for your venue.

01
Comedy Club Shows

Full-night comedy productions for clubs of all sizes. Headliner, feature, and MC packages available. We handle the lineup, logistics, and night-of production.

02
Theater Productions

Polished, high-production comedy shows for performing arts centers and theaters. Professionally staged, marketed, and run from top to bottom.

03
Casino Entertainment

Nationally recognized comedians ready for casino showrooms and lounges. Guaranteed performances with proven headliner talent and full production support.

04

Campus-friendly comedy events tailored for college audiences. We work directly with student programming boards to create unforgettable events.
