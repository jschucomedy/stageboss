About Phil

Powerhouse.Headliner.

Phil Medina is a powerhouse headlining comedian who has performed at top clubs including the Laugh Factory, Hollywood Improv, and the Ice House. Known for his electric stage presence, Phil connects with wide audiences through high-energy, polished performances.

Phil has headlined across the country, entertained U.S. troops, and was featured at the Netflix Is A Joke Festival in Los Angeles. He is also featured on Hulu's West Coast Comedy and Not Your Average Comedy, continuing to build strong national momentum.
