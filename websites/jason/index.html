About

Sharp wit.Real laughs.

Jason Schuster is a bi-coastal stand-up comedian known for his sharp wit, dynamic stage presence, and spot-on impressions. With a unique blend of observational humor and personal anecdotes, Jason seamlessly transitions from clever one-liners to hilarious character impersonations.

Since breaking into the comedy circuit, Jason has performed with some of the biggest names in comedy at the most prestigious clubs in the country — The Comedy Store, Jimmy Kimmel's Comedy Club, The Hollywood Improv, Mic Drop Comedy, Magoobys, and more.

Jason is also a content creator and producer — frequently sharing his humor through social media and podcasts, and producing nationally touring comedy shows through Main Event Comedy Entertainment.

Also: Producer & Tour Manager
